About the Duo

Origin · Pikes Peak

OG2 is a rap duo rooted in the foothills of Colorado Springs. Two MCs, one frequency — raised on mountain air, late-night sessions, and the kind of grind that doesn't show up on a map. The name is a math problem: original, squared. Doubled lineage, doubled output, doubled weight on every bar.

From basement studios off Academy to packed sets on Tejon Street, the duo built a reputation for trading verses with the chemistry of brothers and the precision of a punchline calculator. Hard drums, dust-bowl samples, and writing that takes itself seriously without ever blinking.
